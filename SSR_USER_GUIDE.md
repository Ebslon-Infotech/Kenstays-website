## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is SSR selection mandatory?
**A:** No! All SSR selections are optional. You can skip this step and proceed with just the basic fare.

### Q: Can I modify my SSR selections after booking?
**A:** For LCC airlines, yes! You can use the Air Amendment feature to add SSR after ticketing. For Non-LCC airlines, this may not be available.

### Q: What's the difference between LCC and Non-LCC?
**A:** 
- **LCC (Low-Cost Carriers)**: Airlines like IndiGo, SpiceJet
  - Exact prices shown
  - Immediate confirmation
  - Detailed seat selection
  
- **Non-LCC (Regular Airlines)**: Airlines like Air India, Emirates
  - Preferences only (subject to availability)
  - Confirmation comes later
  - Simple preference selection

### Q: Why don't I see any SSR options?
**A:** Some flights/airlines may not offer SSR services. This is normal and depends on the airline and route.

### Q: Can I select seats for my entire family together?
**A:** Yes! Select seats in the same row or adjacent rows for all passengers.

### Q: What if I don't select any baggage option?
**A:** You'll still get the standard free baggage allowance included in your ticket (if any). SSR baggage is for EXTRA baggage beyond the standard allowance.

### Q: Are meal selections available on all flights?
**A:** Meal options depend on the airline and flight duration. Short domestic flights may have limited or no meal options.

### Q: Can I change my seat after selecting it?
**A:** Yes, during the booking process you can change your selection as many times as you want. Just click on a different seat.

### Q: What happens if my selected seat becomes unavailable?
**A:** For LCC airlines, you'll be notified immediately if a seat is no longer available. For Non-LCC, your preference will be noted but may not be guaranteed.
